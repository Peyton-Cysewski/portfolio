Background

Peyton decided to go the untraditional route of education without a college degree.

After attending a prep school since kindergarten, he attended Washington State University for a few years. The search to find out his purpose in life and in a career led him to try many things including choir and Army ROTC. The thing that resonated with him was computer science.

After several years there, however, it was clear getting an education somewhere else would be more beneficial which led him to attend a coding bootcamp. The time in the bootcamp nourished his adolescent coding prowess. With an undestanding of front end code including HTML, CSS, and Javascript and back end code he was able to make projects for the course and some on his own.
